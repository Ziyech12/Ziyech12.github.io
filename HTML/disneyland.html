Place

            Is a theme park located on reclaimed land in Penny's Bay, Lantau Island. It is located inside the Hong Kong Disneyland Resort and it is owned and managed by Hong Kong International Theme Parks.


How to get there?

            By the Hong Kong International Airport, located on Lantau Island, is easily accessible to Hong Kong Disneyland Resort, with several convenient ways to get to and from the airport.
